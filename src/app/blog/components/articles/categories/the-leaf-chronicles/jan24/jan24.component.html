Thoughts as a first time home-buyer

January 24, 2023
10:47pm

  1. I am house hunting and I want to surprise my parents with the house I choose ~AFTER~ I've bought it to show myself that I can make huge decisions in my life on my own. To stop doubting myself and looking to someone to get approval. To give me confidence to not only reach as far as possible across the stream, but to take a leap across when reaching isn't close enough to the other side. But also to not adopt other people's fears and decision making perspectives.

  2. In a way, buying a house is like a surprise to yourself. Sure, you have options that you know of, but you never know which one will work out. It's like spinning a wheel to see which house it lands on.

  3. Will a house feel as save as this apartment?
